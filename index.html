Destination

Discover the magic of India, where breathtaking landscapes, rich culture, and unforgettable experiences await. From iconic landmarks to hidden gems, this destinations promises to captivate your heart and create memories that last a lifetime.

  • Agra

    Taj Mahal

    A UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the Seven Wonders of the World, this iconic white marble mausoleum is a symbol of eternal love.

  • Punjab

    Amritsar

    Home to the Golden Temple (Harmandir Sahib), a spiritual and architectural marvel. Witness the Wagah Border ceremony for a patriotic experience.

  • Jammu & Kashmir

    Ladakh

    A high-altitude desert with stunning landscapes, crystal-clear lakes (Pangong Tso), and Buddhist monasteries. Perfect for adventure seekers.
